Detox Oasis is a full service fitness, cleanse and weight loss center located on a rustic, family-owned ranch in the remote rolling hills of Southern Indiana.

Detox Oasis retreats offer clients a private, focused, and expertly supervised opportunity to address immediate weight and fitness challenges and start to make lasting, healthy lifestyle changes.

Making healthier food choices and following a regular exercise routine can help improve mood, revitalize energy and help you steer clear of developing possible addictions.

The Ranch

Detox Oasis retreats are held at Elk Ridge Ranch, an authentic farm that has been family owned and operated for over a century. Just outside of French Lick, Elk Ridge Ranch is located in the scenic rolling hills of southern Indiana.

Our fit farm is surrounded by 600 acres of private land and other rural country homes in a secluded and nature-filled environment. Our clients appreciate the bright stars on a clear night and the oxygen-rich fresh air.

This is a get-away that takes you out of your usual day-to-day so you can focus on your goals. There are no distractions, no fast food, and no temptations. It's a natural place to disconnect from the world, and reconnect with yourself.
